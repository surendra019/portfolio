Ludo world
Introducing the Basic Offline Ludo Game MVP, a simple yet engaging version of the classic board game that you can enjoy anytime, anywhere, even without an internet connection. Hit the goal
"Hit the goal" is a unique twist on the classic bubble shooter genre. In this 2D mobile game, player control a football at the bottom of the screen, aiming to launch it accurately towards a central circular goal while navigating a dynamic field of rotating and animated lines.
